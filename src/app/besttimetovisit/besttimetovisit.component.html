Winter Season:

Generally winter is the highest season for tourism in Hyderabad. The temperature holds an amount of 13°C to 25°C during winter. This pleasant weather would perfect for outdoor activities and sightseeing. Winter starts here in the month of November and lasts till February end. The winter receives a large number of tourists during this time in Hyderabad. The Ramojirao film city is the prime attraction of Hyderabad. And you could enjoy and experience the cultural and heritage tourism of Hyderabad.


Summer Season:

Summer holds the longest time in Hyderabad, this starts from March and lasts till July end. March and April have comfortable weather condition and pleasant for outdoor activities and sightseeing. Generally, the temperature range will be between 21°C to 32°C. But this will not hamper your visits insights. May to July considers the hottest time period of Hyderabad. During this time temperature rises up to 41°C. This would be hectic for some travelers those who suffer from health issues.


Monsoon Season:

Monsoon used to reach Hyderabad from August to October. Hyderabad receives moderate to heavy rainfall. This makes the surrounding lush and has a fresh view of its greenery, and the flowers get bloomed at this time to attract more travelers. The average amount of rainfall is 136mm and temperature goes till 20°C to 30°C. Instead of the rain, you will be comfortable with outdoor activities and sightseeing.
